02 About

Designing for clarity in complex systems

I'm a product designer who's spent nearly a decade making complicated, high-stakes software feel simple. At HubSpot I work on AI, payments, and revenue products — the kind where the hard part isn't the pixels, it's the ambiguity: undefined problem spaces, deep technical constraints, and many stakeholders who all need to see themselves in the solution.

I do my best work at 0→1 and at the edges of new technology. I partner closely with engineers, ML teams, PMs, and subject-matter experts to turn dense concepts into intuitive, consumer-grade experiences — and I craft the narratives that get leadership and cross-functional partners aligned behind a vision. I care a lot about trust: software should explain itself and earn confidence.
